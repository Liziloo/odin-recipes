Cindy Reaves' Brownies

Description

These are easily the most delicious, quickest-to-whip-up brownies I've ever made. Throw a few ingredients in a bowl, stir, into the oven! Cocoa powder means you don't even have to mess with melting any chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp (1 stick) melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉.
  2. Grease an 8x8 pan.
  3. Combine all ingredients. Mix until combined.
  4. Pour batter into pan. Bake 30 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.
