Rocky Pasture Fish Chowder

Description

This is another one of my dad's old New England recipes. It comes from Marjorie Mosser's Good Maine Food. It makes a lovely hot meal in the cold months of fall and winter. In Massachusetts, it was also heartening after a rainy and cold summer day out racing Rhodes 19's.

Ingredients

  • 4 sliced potatoes
  • 1 lb diced haddock, cod, striper or halibut (best is smoked finnan haddie)
  • 3 slices salt pork, diced
  • 3 onions, diced
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/4 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 qt whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • large cracker crumbs (we used Pilot crackers, when those existed)

Steps

  1. Boil potato slices in 1 qt water. Drain, reserving all water.
  2. Fry salt pork. Add onions and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add potatoes, curry powder, fish, sugar, milk and cream. Cook over low heat for 30 minutes.
  4. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Top with cracker crumbs.
